5 Best Exercises for Strength

Strength training is an essential part of fitness. Here are the five best exercises to build strength effectively:

  • Squats: Builds lower body strength and core stability.
  • Deadlifts: One of the best full-body exercises.
  • Bench Press: Strengthens chest, shoulders, and triceps.
  • Pull-Ups: Great for back and arm strength.
  • Planks: Improves core strength and endurance.
